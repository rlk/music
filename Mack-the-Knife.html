1. When the shark bites with his teeth, dear,
    scarlet billows start to spread.
    Fancy gloves though wears Macheath, dear,
    so there's not a trace of red.
  2. On the sidewalk, Sunday mornin', baby,
    lies a body, oozin' life.
    Someone's sneakin' around the corner.
    Is the someone, Mack the Knife?
  3. From a tugboat, by the river
    a cement bag's droopin' down.
    And the cement's just for the weight, dear.
    Bet you Mack, he's back in town.
  4. Yeah, Louie Miller, disappeared, dear,
    after drawing out his cash.
    And Macheath spends like a sailor
    did our boy do somethin' rash?
  5. Sukey Tawdry, Jenny Diver
    Lotte Lenya, Sweet Lucy Brown.
    Yes, the line forms on the right, dears,
    now that Mack, he's back in town.
